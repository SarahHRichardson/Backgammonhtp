Playing the Game
Once the game is set up and the first turn is completed, the players can begin playing backgammon in earnest.
Types of Rolls
There are three common types of rolls in Backgammon - the standard roll, doubles, and acey-deucey (optional). Each roll is explained in the figure below.
Capture
When a player lands on a space that contains one chip of the opposite player's color, the player captures that piece.
Bearing Off
Once all chips are in a player's home quadrant, they can bear off - or take their chips off the backgammon board - to win the game.

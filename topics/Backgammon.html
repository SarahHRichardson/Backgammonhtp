Backgammon

Backgammon is a board game.

Backgammon is one of the oldest known games in the world. The first variation of the game surfaced around 5,000 years ago, and many, many ancient cultures played and contributed to the game.

As a result, many, many variations of Backgammon exist. The purpose of this website is to teach the most common way to play Backgammon, although I have catalogued one variation, Acey-Deucey, at the end of the site as an example of the ways that Backgammon's rules are manipulated and modified.

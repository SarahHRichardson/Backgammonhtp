Required Game Pieces

Any backgammon set comes with several different pieces. To set up the game, you must be able to tell between these pieces.

Table 1. Necessary Backgammon Pieces
Piece Name Description Image
Backgammon Board The backgammon board is rectangular with triangles (game spaces) facing towards the horizontal middle of the board. The board will close on the vertical middle of the board, effectively separating the board into four different sections, each with six triangles.

Chips

There are 15 Backgammon chips in two different colors, making 30 total chips. Each player will be assigned to one color. Two chips of each color are depicted here.

Dice Each player will have two standard 6-sided dice (although both players can also share one set). The color of each dice should reflect the player's chip colors. Some backgammon sets may also come with cups to shake the dice in.

Doubling Die Beyond the sets of dice above, there is another die, called the doubling die, that has the numbers 2, 4, 8, 16, 32 and 64 inscribed. The use of this die in backgammon is optional. See Points for more information.
